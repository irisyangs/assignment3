My Trip to Alaska

I went on a trip to Alaska last year. I loved driving by on the roads and seeing the glaciers and wildlife. We also went kayaking in the ocean and saw sea otters and bald eagles. It was a really fun trip and I hope to go back again someday!

My Favorite Restaurant

Molly Tea

Although this place is not a restaurant, this is my favorite tea store. I love the jasmine and fruit teas they serve here, and it's refreshingly sweet - perfect for a hot day!

My Favorite Food

Strawberries are my favorite fruit! I love eating them fresh, and they are also delicious in smoothies and desserts. They are sweet and juicy, and they're a must-have addition to my breakfast meal and any dessert I have.
